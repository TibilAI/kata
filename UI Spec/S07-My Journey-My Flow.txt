# Flow Engine v1

The Flow Engine measures the user's practice consistency and quality of reflection.

It has three independent outputs:

1. Flow Level
2. Next Upgrade Guidance
3. Flow Index

---

# 1. Flow Level

Flow Level represents the user's current stage of deliberate practice.

There are three levels.

| Level | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Shodan | Beginning a reflective practice |
| Rensha | Consistent practitioner |
| Dōsha | Deliberate practitioner with clear thinking habits |

A user can only move forward one level at a time.

Downgrades are not automatic.

---

## Promotion Rules

### Shodan → Rensha

Minimum evidence

• Complete at least 21 Daily practices

AND

• Complete at least 3 Weekly practices

AND

• Complete 1 Monthly practice

AND

• Average Flow Index ≥ 60

AND

• No gap longer than 7 consecutive days

---

### Rensha → Dōsha

Minimum evidence

• Complete additional 60 Daily practices

AND

• Complete additional 8 Weekly practices

AND

• Complete additional 3 Monthly practices

AND

• Average Flow Index ≥ 80

AND

• No gap longer than 14 consecutive days

---

# 2. Next Upgrade Guidance

Guidance is NOT AI generated.

It is rule based.

The engine analyses every completed Practice.

---

Each question belongs to one capability.

| Capability | Questions |
|------------|-----------|
| Understanding Problems | D1 W1 M1 |
| Challenging Assumptions | D2 |
| Decision Making | D3 M4 |
| Creating Impact | D4 W2 M7 |
| Continuous Improvement | D5 W3 M8 |

# V1 Philosophy

Flow rewards

• consistency

• reflection

• deliberate thinking

It does NOT reward

• writing longer answers

• emotions

• AI judged quality

• productivity

• output

The goal is to reward the habit of thinking.
